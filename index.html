Why Do We Need a Makerspace?

Growing up in Pakistan, education was primarily textbook-driven, with little to no hands-on learning. Science labs were restrictive, where even touching equipment required permission, and practicals often meant copying from a book rather than conducting real experiments. This lack of experiential learning stifled creativity and problem-solving skills.

Through opportunities to work and study abroad, I explored co-working spaces, entrepreneurial hubs, and makerspaces in countries like the USA, Australia, Kenya, and the UK. These spaces were vibrant hubs of creativity—places where people collaborated, co-designed, prototyped, and turned ideas into reality. Inspired by these experiences, I envisioned bringing the same culture of innovation to Pakistan, starting with the first makerspace in Gilgit-Baltistan.

Building the First Makerspace in Gilgit-Baltistan

Nestled in one of the most breathtaking yet geographically challenging regions of Pakistan, Gilgit-Baltistan faces limited access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. The climate crisis, poverty, and inadequate infrastructure further isolate communities, making sustainable living an urgent necessity.

Makeistan aims to address these challenges by establishing a makerspace that fosters problem-solving through design thinking, entrepreneurship, and practical skill development. This space will serve as a hub for local teams to work on community-driven projects, such as:

  • Redesigning traditional stoves for better efficiency and reduced environmental impact
  • Revitalizing and innovating Pamir homes to enhance sustainability
  • Building small wind turbines to provide renewable energy to households
  • Using technology to protect glaciers and combat climate change

Additionally, we will empower women artisans engaged in jewelry-making, handicrafts, and furniture design by equipping them with tools, training, and entrepreneurial support. This will enable them to scale their businesses and create sustainable livelihoods.

At Makeistan, we believe in the power of making, learning, and innovating together. Join us in building a future where creativity drives real-world impact, and where every challenge presents an opportunity for innovation.
