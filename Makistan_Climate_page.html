* Makeistan proposes the establishment of a new, dedicated unit titled the Makeistan Climate Innovation Lab (MCIL). The lab will focus solely on addressing the multifaceted challenges posed by climate change in Pakistan, especially in vulnerable regions like Gilgit-Baltistan. MCIL aims to merge local wisdom with modern technology to co-create sustainable, scalable, and inclusive climate solutions.*
